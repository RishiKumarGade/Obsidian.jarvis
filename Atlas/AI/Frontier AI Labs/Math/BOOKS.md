
### Linear Algebra

**Schaum's Outline of Linear Algebra** — Seymour Lipschutz & Marc Lipson
    - Focus: Build computational speed with hundreds of solved and unsolved problems.
- **Introduction to Linear Algebra** — Gilbert Strang
    - Focus: Develop geometric intuition and understand how linear algebra is used in engineering and machine learning.
- **Linear Algebra Done Right** — Sheldon Axler
    - Focus: Gain rigorous mathematical understanding and proof-writing skills.
- **Matrix Analysis** — Roger A. Horn & Charles R. Johnson
    - Focus: Advanced matrix theory, eigenvalue analysis, matrix inequalities, and spectral methods.
- **Numerical Linear Algebra** — Lloyd N. Trefethen & David Bau III
    - Focus: Learn the algorithms used in scientific computing and machine learning (QR, SVD, iterative methods, conditioning, numerical stability).
